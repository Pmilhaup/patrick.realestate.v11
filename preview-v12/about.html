Chapter One The Practice

Tailored service.
Institutional discipline.

The practice is built the way a research desk is built: a daily reconciliation between what the data says and what the conversation says. Notre Dame — BA Psychology, 2007. University of San Diego — MS Real Estate, 2012. Then AvalonBay in Los Angeles, Alliant Capital, World Class Holdings in Austin, HG Capital in Menlo Park. Institutional asset management, then luxury brokerage at Sotheby's beginning 2016. Every listing call now opens with a live dashboard pulled that morning from the MLS — the same analytical posture, applied to the most storied homes on the North Shore.

The result is a practice that looks intimate from the outside (one advisor, one calendar) and runs like an institutional desk on the inside (a 6,335-property predictability model, a daily MLS snapshot, a custom CMA pipeline, a pre-MLS off-market network). Tailored service on the front of house. Quant rigor in the back office. Both.

Chapter Two Jameson Sotheby's International Realty

The brand.
The corridor.

Jameson Sotheby's International Realty is the Chicago affiliate of the Sotheby's International Realty global network — founded in 1976 inside the venerable auction house (est. 1744), today connecting 1,100+ offices across 86 countries and territories with the most-recognized brand in luxury real estate. For represented homes, the marketing footprint runs from a private pre-launch through global syndication into the Wall Street Journal Mansion section, the New York Times, the Financial Times, Architectural Digest, Mansion Global, Bloomberg, RESIDE® Magazine, the Houseguest video series, and the only real-estate app on Apple TV. For discerning buyers, the same network is a gateway to homes that are never publicly advertised — a quiet, curated pipeline measured in days, not weeks.

The Sotheby's editorial work has been cited on this practice. Crain's Chicago Business and The Real Deal have covered the listings, including the 999 Lake Road episode (the on-the-record source on the Caleb Williams story) and the Harris estate at 2441 Woodbridge Lane. The brand opens the door; the practice does the work.
