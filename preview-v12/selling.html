01
Chapter One The Practice

Provenance to close.

STEP · 01
Provenance & Architectural Research
Original architect, build date, ownership chain, restoration history, comparable sales of similarly authored homes. The storied narrative your buyer will read on the listing is built here.
STEP · 02
Reconciliation Pricing
Three independent methods — sales comparison, replacement cost, income-equivalent. The number we bring to market is the one all three agree on.
STEP · 03
Photography & Long-form Narrative
Golden-hour exterior, twilight pool, interior across two weather days. Five hundred words of architectural narrative, artfully composed. No bullet points.
STEP · 04
ZenList Pre-Market Window
Seven to ten days of private exposure inside the Sotheby's pre-MLS network before public listing — frequently enough to introduce the right buyer before the home ever reaches broad market.
STEP · 05
MLS & Open-House Choreography
When we go to broad market: three open houses across three weather conditions, two private brokers' opens, and a curated invite list of forty Sotheby's advisors with active matched buyers.
STEP · 06
Closing Discipline
Attorney review, inspection negotiation, appraisal defense, closing-day audit. Forty-nine line items on the closing checklist. None of them skipped.
