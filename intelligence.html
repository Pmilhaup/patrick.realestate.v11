Chapter Four Method

Two sources.
One reconciled timeline.

Pre-March 2026 the historical baseline comes from Infosparks (the MRED long-run reference series). From March 2026 forward, the dashboard reads directly from the live MLS — a daily snapshot piped through the MLS Database backend and reconciled against the Infosparks tail. The vertical gold dashed line on every five-year chart marks the handoff. Volatility before the marker is monthly; after, it's daily.
